Scope & sequence

Scope & Sequence

The six breakouts build one durable idea across grades 3–8: AI makes guesses from patterns in data, it can be confidently wrong, so you verify. Each grade adds the next layer of that arc.

The learning arc: pattern & prediction (Gr 3) → data quality & bias-from-data (Gr 4) → clear prompting & verifying (Gr 5) → spotting generated media (Gr 6) → where bias comes from (Gr 7) → creating responsibly (Gr 8). Vocabulary compounds — data introduced in Gr 3–4 returns inside training data and bias in Gr 7.

Pacing

  • One breakout ≈ 25–40 minutes including debrief; it works best as the engage or collaborative-practice phase of a lesson, not the whole lesson.
  • Run one per grading period, or cluster a band into a 1–2 week AI-literacy unit.
  • Grades 3–5 are free and self-contained; Grades 6–8 are the licensed band and assume the earlier vocabulary.
